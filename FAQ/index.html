? What are contact credits?

Contact credits are the keys to unlock the contact details. If you are a tutor looking for students, you can use contact credits to see the contact details of student/parent who posted the tuition needs. If you are student/parent, you can use contact credits to contact tutors whom you like.

? What are the benefits of taking premium membership?

Premium membership offers many benefits to the student.
a) Premium students can see the contact details (mobile, email and address) of the tutors they like.
b) The tuitions (learning needs) posted by premium students will be listed as featured tuitions which attract more quality tutors.
c) They can get SMS alert when a student from his locality posts tuition in his subject.
d) We advertise more and reach out to more tutors in the areas where there are more premium students.
e) You will get more reward points.
f) Special attention till you find the best tutor.
